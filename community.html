Faq

  • 01

    Will you be getting more stock?

    We do our best to restock popular items however, in the case of limited editions this is not always possible. We currently don't have a 'notify me'-service available to let you know when an item is back in stock. But if you would like to ask about a product, size or color that is not shown, please contact our customer care team.

  • 02

    Are your jeans unisex?

    Yes! All our fits are unisex. However, we have launched some fits that are specifically designed for women, these fits are Hightop Tilde, Breezy Britt, Straight Sally, Lofty Lo and Clean Eileen. That said, these still work great for guys as well.

  • 03

    What leather do you use?

    The leather used for accessories is of European origin, while the leather used for jackets is of Indian and Middle Eastern origin.We have required our suppliers to limit the souring of raw hides from certain areas/countries. For our leather accessories, we only use vegetable tanned leather.

  • 04

    Can I purchase a gift card online?

    At the moment this is not possible, but we're working on being able to offer this service in the future!

  • 05

    Do you ship to my country?

    We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Your current shipping destination is shown inside the menu on the bottom left of our website.
